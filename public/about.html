Glory Days

Americana from Central IL

 Glory Days was started in 2008 as a tribute to the music of Bruce Springsteen and the E Street Band. As a Springsteen Tribute we have played throughout the Midwest over the past few years playing such events as the Taste Of Chicago, Ribfest in Naperville, The Castle Theater, The Lincoln Theater, Decatur Celebration, Battle Creek MI Field of Flight and many more, along with numerous corporate functions. Over the past 2 years Glory Days has expanded into a Classic Rock, Americana and Funk party band as well... so whether you are looking for the best Springsteen Tribute available or a good time party band, Glory Days will deliver.
